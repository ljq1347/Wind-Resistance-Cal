# Solar Street Light Structure & Wind Load Calculator (V1.0 Beta)

This repository hosts a specialized, open-source structural engineering tool tailored for the global outdoor lighting and solar PV industries. It is designed to evaluate and verify the wind resistance and structural safety of solar street lights and integrated smart pole systems under varying wind loading conditions.

## 🚀 Core Features
* **Full-Component Parameterization**: Supports inputting detailed geometric and physical metadata for poles, brackets, solar modules, battery enclosures, and lighting fixtures.
* **Automated Wind Load Verification**: Processes component datasets against localized standard wind speeds to automatically compute stress distribution, structural displacement, and overturning safety indices based on mainstream international codes, driving precise digital estimation and hardware pre-selection during preliminary engineering phases.

## ⚠️ Beta Phase & Disclaimer
This is an **Open Beta (V1.0)** release. The underlying engineering algorithms and mathematical models are under continuous refinement and iteration. Developed by the engineering division at opensolardesign.com, this tool is intended strictly for preliminary structural estimation, R&D reference, and engineering validation. It should not be used as the sole or absolute metric for final localized structural compliance.
